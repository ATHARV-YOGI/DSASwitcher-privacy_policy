Privacy Policy – DSASwitcher

Last Updated: April 19, 2025

Thank you for using DSASwitcher – a Chrome extension that helps coders stay focused by redirecting them to curated DSA problems from trusted sources like LeetCode and GeeksforGeeks.

🔒 What Data Do We Collect?

None. DSASwitcher does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or identifiable user information.

📦 Local Storage Only

We use Chrome's storage.local to save your extension preferences on your device, including:

  • Your selected DSA sheet
  • Daily goal count
  • Progress (questions completed today)
  • Whether redirection is enabled

This data is stored locally in your browser and is not sent anywhere.

🔐 Permissions

DSASwitcher uses the following permissions:

  • "storage" – to store extension settings and progress.
  • "tabs" – to redirect the current tab to a DSA question.
  • "webNavigation" – to detect when a user opens a new page and trigger redirection.

These are used solely for core functionality. We do not monitor, track, or inject anything into websites you visit.

🌐 External Websites

This extension redirects to trusted coding platforms like:

  • LeetCode
  • GeeksforGeeks

We do not access or store any data from these sites. Refer to their privacy policies for more information.

📉 No Analytics, Ads, or Trackers

  • No Google Analytics
  • No cookies or fingerprinting
  • No ads or third-party scripts
